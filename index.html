MagTv



We are MagTv ~ a creative team focused on Entertainment, Diversity, & Fun ~ Our
filming crew has been working hard this school year providing photography for school events
such as sports games as well as our notorious MagTv daily announcements.

MagTv was founded in 2021 by the students in Multimedia 2 and lead by instructor Sean Manning.



Making content isn't easy. It takes time, effort, and execution.
But when you're in the flow, it is incredibly rewarding.

Here is a youtube video showing MagTv's execution in the daily announcements.



Magtv films daily to provide up to date information for announcments to the students at
Magnolia high school. The casting team involves students who are determined and open to
new experiences and most of all willing to have some fun while filming.

There are several positions that students fill. For example there is the Director, Cameraman, Head of
Music, as well as the pair of students who appear on camera. All these components are vital to creating great film and
the students certainly exceed expectations.
